Aurin Basecamp

A research facility built upon and around a wheat field 20 minutes southwest of Nocturne City. Expeditions have been sent out through a cross-dimensional gateway found there, and bringing back those willing to flee the wasteland on the other side. After which, subjects are decontaminated, supplied with various vaccines, and watched for body composition changes over two months until being moved to another section of the facility. Heather Ouras, lacking a surname at the time of her intake to Aurin, was the first documented rescue. Upon entering the vicinity of the active gateway, their body began changing composition. While previously slightly amorphous, exposure to a decontamination bay in the unknown alternate world had resulted in her body taking a permanent shape.
