Innovation's Reach

PLANETARY SYSTEM: Tectis 6 PLANET: [DECRYPTING...]

A centralized base for Ezra Lomura's work in engineering and life. It is situated on a planet in the Tectis system; an atmospheric planet with a forest that spans a chunk of the solid surface. It never stops raining, but it fluctuates from a drizzle to a torrential downpour. Additionally, the forest constantly has a slight fogginess to it. Innovation's Reach Workshop is made up of three domes with reinforced-glass for much of the surface area. One dome is for her resting area, another for general use like food preparation, and the third, largest, has a workspace for personal weapons and suits, with an underground docking bay for any ships that Ezra works on. The planet otherwise is as close to untouched as possible. Ezra Lomura recieves weekly shipments from her original home world's production facilities to the Workshop. These shipments contain miscellaneous items, food, and any made-to-order parts.
