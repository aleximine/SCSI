Innovation's Reach

> Address - PLANET 6 | TECTIS SYSTEM (custom entry, unverified) > Ownership - Independent, owned by Ezra Lomura > Property Tax - Unknown, likely Residential Housing

A centralized base for Ezra Lomura's work in engineering and life. It is situated on a planet in the Tectis system; an atmospheric planet with a forest that spans a chunk of the solid surface. It never stops raining, but it fluctuates from a drizzle to a torrential downpour. Additionally, the forest constantly has a slight fogginess to it. Innovation's Reach Workshop is made up of three domes with reinforced-glass for much of the surface area. One dome is for her resting area, another for general use like food preparation, and the third, largest, has a workspace for personal weapons and suits, with an underground docking bay for any ships that Ezra works on. The planet otherwise is as close to untouched as possible. Ezra Lomura recieves weekly shipments from her original home world's production facilities to the Workshop. These shipments contain miscellaneous items, food, and any made-to-order parts.

[Habitation Dome]

The habitation dome is the primary living space for the workshop’s occupant or occupants. It consists of sleeping quarters with a very large bed, a bathing/shower area, closets, and storage. This is the primary R&R space, and smallest in size compared to the other two.

[Recreation Dome]

The recreation dome consists of a kitchen, living space, and a miniature fitness center. Occupants prepare food, the ingredients of which are replenished on a weekly shipment of supplies much like parts for the engineering bay. This dome middles in size compared to the other two domes.

[Workshop Dome and Engineering Bay]

The workshop dome is the largest of the three by no small margin. This dome’s glass is able to fully retract to allow access to a landing area in the middle for vessels that require work. Underneath, accessed by the landing area’s retractable surface, there is a hangar for any such vessels to be taken to for engineering work to be completed. The top level is dedicated towards personal weapons and suit modifications, with the landing pad often being converted to a convenient firing range for weapons testing.
